# 💼 CORPORATE RAIDER SIMULATOR

> *"In the game of power… there are no rules, only winners."*

A browser-based strategy game set in the **late 1990s** — the golden age of greed, market speculation and hostile takeovers. Build a financial empire, manage debt and stress, and reach **$500M in net monthly profit** before the authorities catch up with you.

**[▶ PLAY NOW](https://mragnacci.github.io/Corporate-Game/)**

## 🎮 Gameplay

Each turn represents one month. You must:

- **SCAN** the market to find acquisition targets
- **BUY** companies with cash or via LBO (leveraged buyout)
- **MANAGE** debt, stress and legal risk
- **REACT** to breaking news and random events
- **HIRE** staff and buy luxury assets to gain passive bonuses
- Reach **$500M net monthly profit** to win

## ⚙️ Features

- 🖥️ **Windows 95 aesthetic** — full retro UI with authentic look & feel
- 📊 **Full income statement** — Revenue, COGS, EBIT, taxes, net income
- 🏦 **Bank system** — borrow and repay debt, dynamic interest rates
- 💎 **Lifestyle catalog** — Gold Watch, Exclusive Club, Private Jet, Private Island
- 📰 **Breaking news** — market events to react to each turn
- ⚡ **Random events** — strikes, scandals, crises
- 🕵️ **Insider trading** — pay for market intelligence (at your legal risk)
- 👥 **Staff management** — hire CFO, COO, PR to gain bonuses
- 🏆 **Records system** — local leaderboard saved in your browser
- 🤖 **AI Board of Directors** — powered by Google Gemini (optional)

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

This is a **single-file web app** with zero dependencies to install:

- [React 18](https://react.dev/) (via CDN)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) (via CDN)
- [Babel Standalone](https://babeljs.io/) (JSX in browser)
- Google Gemini API *(optional, for AI advisor feature)*

---

## 🤖 AI Advisor (Optional)

The **CDA** (Board of Directors) feature uses Google Gemini to give you cynical strategic advice.

To enable it:
1. Get a free API key from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/)
2. Open `index.html` and find this line:
   ```js
   const apiKey = "";
   ```
3. Paste your key between the quotes and save
